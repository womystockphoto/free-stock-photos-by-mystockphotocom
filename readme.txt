Find a perfect stock photo from over 3 million free photos on MyStockPhoto.com and insert it to your post directly by just a few clicks.

== Description ==

MyStockPhoto.com has a collection of 3,000,000+ free stock photos. This plugin enables you to find a perfect stock photo and quickly insert it to your blog post without leaving post editor. It's smooth, 100% free and no registration required.

= Feature Highlights =

* More than 3,000,000 free stock photos to choose from
* Choose to host stock photo files on our server or download to your server
* Instant photo resizer and previewer outputs a final stock photo at any dimensions
* Automatically crop your stock photos using pre-defined aspect ratios to (original, 1:1, 4:3 and 3:4)
* Show/hide/custom border around the photos when inserted into posts.
* Stock Photo description in 17 languages

== Installation ==

1. Upload the files to the `{www}/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Go to Tools->Import and use the 'Free Stock Photos by MyStockPhoto.com'.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I host the photos on my own server =
Yes, you have an option to download the final photo to your server. Or you may host it on our server and hot link to the photo in order to save bandwidth.

= Can I remove the stock photo attribution from a creative commons photo? =
Only if you get special permission from the author.

= Who own's the Creative Common's photos? =
The original photographer owns the rights to their stock photos and by using it you are bound by the Creative Common's License.
